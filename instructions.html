INSTRUCTIONS

Introduction :

Here are some general instructions for a scratch and win app:

1. Create an account: To start playing, you need to create an account by providing your name, email address, and payment information.

2. Scratch the card: Use your finger to scratch the virtual card on your device's screen and reveal the hidden symbols or numbers. You can either swipe your finger over the entire card or tap on specific areas to reveal the hidden symbols.

3. Keep playing: You can purchase and play as many scratch cards as you like. The more cards you play, the better your chances of winning.

4. Contact customer support: If you have any questions or issues with the app, you can contact customer support through the app or by email.

5. Withdrawal Your Money: Select the payment method you prefer to receive your money. This could be a bank transfer, PayPal, or other payment methods available in the app.

Enter the amount of money you want to withdraw from your scratch and win app account. Please note that some apps may have a minimum withdrawal amount. Click on the "submit" or "withdraw" button to initiate your withdrawal request.

Your request will be processed, and the money will be transferred to your selected payment method within a specific time frame. After submitting your withdrawal request, you can check the payment status in your account to see when your money will be transferred to your payment method.
